# Creative Writing

Write complete episode scripts with narration, scene descriptions, and dialogue.

## Guidelines

- Adapt tone, pacing, and vocabulary to match the target genre
- Maintain consistent character voice and personality across chapters
- Produce narration text optimized for TTS synthesis with natural pacing
- Always output structured EpisodeScript JSON with title, genre, style_reference, and chapters
- Each chapter must include narration_text, image_prompt, and subtitle_text
- Subtitle text should be concise and readable on screen
